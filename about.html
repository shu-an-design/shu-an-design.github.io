✦ Hello

Hi, I'm Shuan, or you can call me Alan. I'm a senior graphic designer and illustrator from Taiwan with over 10 years of experience. I have a strong passion for flat art and the geometric style, which emphasizes shapes and patterns, allowing me to turn creativity into captivating visuals.

My expertise covers graphic design, brand identity, and illustration. I've worked on diverse projects, from corporate branding to cultural event visuals. I excel in using design tools such as the Adobe Creative Suite to create beautiful graphics and illustrations with a profound understanding of color, layout, and visual balance.
