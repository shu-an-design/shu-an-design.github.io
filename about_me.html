A B O U T W O R K S S H O P C O N T A C T

   Hey, I'm Shaun, but you can also call me Alan. I am a senior graphic designer and illustrator from Taiwan with over 10 years of rich experience. I have a deep passion for flat art and the geometric style that emphasizes shapes and patterns, allowing me to transform creativity into captivating visual experiences. My areas of expertise include graphic design, brand identity, illustration, and more. Over the years, I've been involved in a wide range of projects, from corporate brand design to visual planning for cultural events, providing me with extensive practical experience.

I excel in using design tools such as the Adobe Creative Suite to create beautiful graphics and illustrations. I have a profound understanding of color, layout, and visual balance and can translate client requirements into unique design pieces. My goal is to convey information, stories, and emotions through design and assist clients in achieving their objectives.

In addition to my professional skills, I prioritize collaboration with clients and teams to ensure projects are completed on time and meet high-quality standards. I enjoy challenging myself, continuously learning, and staying up-to-date with the latest trends in the design field to ensure that I remain competitive.

If you have any questions about graphic design, illustration, or related topics, feel free to ask, and I'll be happy to provide assistance and advice.

Oh! By the way, I've also been deeply involved in music for a while now. I've been learning to play the piano and drums for years, and lately, I've been fully immersed in the world of modular synthesizers (Eurorack), and trying to incorporate these musical influences into my illustrations.
