A B O U T I L L U S T R A T I O N S H O P C O N T A C T

    Hey, I'm Shaun, but you can also call me Alan. I'm a senior graphic designer and illustrator from Taiwan with over 10 years of experience. I have a strong passion for flat art and the geometric style that highlights shapes and patterns. Most of my artistic inspiration comes from everyday life and the things that capture my attention. By the way, I've also been deeply involved in music for a while now. I've been learning to play the piano and drums, but lately, I've been fully immersed in the world of modular synthesizers (Eurorack). I'm trying to incorporate these musical influences into my illustrations.
